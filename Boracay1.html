Boracay

Boracay is one of the most famous beaches in the Philippines, known for its powdery white sands and vibrant nightlife. Located in Aklan province, it attracts both local and international tourists year-round. Boracay offers a wide range of activities, from water sports like kite surfing and scuba diving to relaxing sunset cruises.

The stunning White Beach in Boracay is divided into three sections: Station 1 for luxury resorts, Station 2 for shopping and dining, and Station 3 for more budget-friendly accommodations. Boracay has consistently been rated as one of the best islands in the world.
