Coron

Coron town’s attractions are as diverse as they are spectacular. For divers, the island offers a rare experience: exploring sunken Japanese warships from World War II, now transformed into thriving coral reefs filled with vibrant marine life. These underwater relics are among the most famous wreck diving sites in the world, making Coron town a paradise for diving enthusiasts visiting the Philippines. Above water, the town of Coron is equally mesmerizing. With island hopping tours, you can visit breathtaking spots like the Twin Lagoons, Kayangan Lake, and Barracuda Lake are some of the town’s most stunning spots, with turquoise waters surrounded by towering karst cliffs. Hidden beaches and secluded islands add even more to Coron town's allure, making it a dream destination for relaxation and adventure alike.

In this guide, you'll uncover everything Coron town has to offer—from the best times to visit and top activities to recommended places to eat and stay. Whether you’re here for the adventure or the tranquility, Coron town promises an unforgettable escape into one of the Philippines’ most captivating destinations.
