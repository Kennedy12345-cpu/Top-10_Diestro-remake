Panglao Island

Panglao Island is Bohol's best beach destination. It is situated southwest of Bohol and south of Cebu. Suarez Bridge and Gov. Borja Bridge which links Panglao to Bohol's main island. It includes two municipalities: Dauis and Panglao. Alona Beach and Dumaluan Beach are situated in the Panglao municipality. Most of Bohol's top hotels and resorts are on Panglao Island. Most sun, sea, and sand lovers stay in this part of the province due to its proximity to pristine beaches and islands.

The island's top beach destination with most luxury hotels, restaurants, and bars is Alona Beach. Renowned for its long stretch of white sand and crystal-clear waters. You should try water activities like parasailing, banana boat, fly fish, and UFO trip aside from working on your tan and doing different activities.
