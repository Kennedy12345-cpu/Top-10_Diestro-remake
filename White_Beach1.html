White Beach (Puerto Galera)

Puerto Galera is one of the three places in the Philippines recognized by UNESCO as a Man and Biosphere Reserve in 1973, the other two are Palawan and Albay. Aside from its white beaches, it is also known to be a popular diving site, boasting precious marine life. There is also a multitude of resorts here for rest and relaxation that can fit any budget.
